# 🌟 Project Overview

The **Insurance Risk & Claims Analysis Dashboard** is a comprehensive **Power BI Business Intelligence project** developed to analyze:

✔️ Insurance claim trends  
✔️ Customer risk behavior  
✔️ Vehicle-related claims  
✔️ Coverage zone performance  
✔️ Demographic insights  
✔️ Claim amount distributions  

This dashboard helps insurance companies make **data-driven decisions** by transforming raw insurance data into meaningful business insights.

# 📊 Dashboard Insights

## 🚘 Claim Amount by Car Use
- Private vehicles generate the highest claims.
- Commercial usage contributes comparatively lower claims.

---

## 🏭 Claim Amount by Car Make
- Brands like **Ford**, **Chevrolet**, and **Toyota** contribute significantly to total claim amounts.

---

## 🌍 Claim Amount by Coverage Zone
- Urban and Rural zones have nearly equal claim distributions.
- Highly Urban regions show increased insurance activity.

---

## 👥 Claim Amount by Age Group
- Middle-aged customers contribute the highest claims.
- Younger and senior groups show lower claim amounts.

---

## 📅 Claim Amount by Car Year
- Modern vehicles have higher claim values.
- Older vehicles contribute comparatively smaller claims.

---

## 🎓 Claim Amount by Education
- Customers with Bachelor's and Master's degrees generate higher claims.

---

## 💍 Claim Amount by Marital Status
- Married customers contribute the highest insurance claim amounts.
